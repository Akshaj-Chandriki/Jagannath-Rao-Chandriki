Jagannath Rao Venkata Rao Chandriki

Freedom Fighter • Lawyer • Minister • Parliamentarian

Biography (English)

Introduction

Jagannath Rao Venkata Rao Chandriki (16 August 1916 – 20th century) was a distinguished Indian freedom fighter, jurist, administrator, and parliamentarian who made significant contributions to the political, social, and legal development of Hyderabad State and later Mysore State, which is now Karnataka. Belonging to the generation of leaders who seamlessly bridged the gap between colonial India and independent India, Chandriki’s life was marked by integrity, dedication, and service to the nation.

Historical Context

Born during the era of British colonial rule and the princely state governance of the Nizam of Hyderabad, Chandriki witnessed firsthand the challenges of regional autonomy, social inequality, and political oppression. The early 20th century in Hyderabad was a period of intense social stratification, limited education for common citizens, and restricted political freedoms. Growing up in such an environment, young Chandriki developed a keen awareness of civic responsibility, justice, and nationalism.

Early Life and Family Background

Jagannath Rao Chandriki was born in Chandriki village of Gulbarga district, Hyderabad State, to Shri Venkata Rao Chandriki, a man renowned for his integrity, discipline, and commitment to public welfare. His mother, a deeply religious and compassionate woman, instilled in him moral values, empathy, and a sense of social duty. The family was well-respected in the local community for their educational background and ethical standing.

Chandriki’s formative years were shaped by the dual influences of traditional Indian culture and emerging nationalist ideals. He witnessed the struggles of ordinary citizens under the feudal structures and British influence, and these experiences inspired him to contribute to the cause of social justice and national freedom.

Education and Intellectual Growth

Chandriki pursued his primary and secondary education in local schools, displaying exceptional aptitude in literature, history, and political thought. Recognizing his talent and commitment, his family supported his higher education. He enrolled at Nagpur University, where he obtained a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) degree. Thereafter, he pursued a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) at Osmania University, Hyderabad, which provided him with a strong foundation in legal theory, constitutional principles, and judicial procedures.

During his university years, Chandriki developed an enduring interest in political philosophy, history, and public administration. He participated in student debates, literary societies, and discussions on civil liberties, which honed his analytical skills and laid the groundwork for his future political and legal career.

Participation in the Freedom Movement

The 1940s were a tumultuous period in India’s struggle for independence. Chandriki actively participated in the Quit India Movement of 1942 and other nationalist activities in Hyderabad State. In a region controlled by the Nizam, expressing anti-colonial sentiments or organizing political movements was fraught with risk. Undeterred, Chandriki organized secret meetings, distributed nationalist literature, and mobilized youth support for the independence cause.

His fearless engagement in these activities led to multiple arrests and periods of imprisonment. These sacrifices earned him respect as a dedicated freedom fighter and established his reputation as a principled leader willing to place the nation above personal comfort or safety.

Legal Career and Advocacy

After completing his law degree, Chandriki embarked on a legal career as an advocate. He quickly gained recognition for his rigorous analysis, ethical approach, and commitment to justice. His practice focused on civil law, public interest cases, and matters related to social equity. He often represented individuals from marginalized communities, advocating for their rights and challenging inequitable practices.

His legal experience provided him with invaluable insights into the functioning of the judiciary, legislative frameworks, and administrative processes, which later proved crucial in his political and ministerial roles.

Political Career in Hyderabad State

Following India’s independence in 1947 and the subsequent integration of Hyderabad State into the Indian Union, Chandriki emerged as a prominent political leader. He was elected as a Member of the Hyderabad State Legislative Assembly (1951–1956). His tenure coincided with the period of state consolidation, where administrative structures were being aligned with democratic principles.

As Minister for Law and Endowment, Chandriki was instrumental in reforming religious endowments, codifying legal procedures, and establishing frameworks for transparent governance. He balanced traditional expectations with modern administrative needs, ensuring that the reforms were accepted by both the public and political peers.

Role After State Reorganisation

The States Reorganisation Act of 1956 merged parts of Hyderabad State with Mysore State. Chandriki continued his public service in the reorganized state, assuming portfolios such as Excise, Labour, and Small Scale Industries. He prioritized industrial development in backward regions, labor welfare, and promotion of small-scale enterprises to generate employment. His policies reflected a commitment to inclusive growth and equitable distribution of resources.

Member of Parliament (Lok Sabha) Contributions

In 1962, Chandriki was elected to the 3rd Lok Sabha from the Raichur constituency as an Indian National Congress candidate. In Parliament, he actively participated in debates, particularly on legal reforms, labor policies, and economic development. He advocated for infrastructural development in his constituency and emphasized policies for social upliftment of marginalized groups. His contributions were widely acknowledged for their depth, clarity, and pragmatism.

Personal Life and Hobbies

Chandriki married Shrimati Vimalabai in 1938, and together they had one son and three daughters. Despite a demanding public life, he maintained close ties with his family and led a disciplined, modest lifestyle. He was an avid reader, interested in history, law, political philosophy, and biographies of global leaders. He also engaged in writing essays and speeches that inspired youth and fellow politicians.

Leadership Philosophy and Ideology

Chandriki’s leadership philosophy was deeply rooted in ethical governance, public service, and constitutional principles. He believed in the democratic process, secular values, and social equity. Power, for him, was a tool to serve society, not a means for personal gain. His quiet, principled style of leadership earned him respect across party lines.

Legacy and Historical Significance

Jagannath Rao Chandriki’s legacy is reflected in the legal, political, and administrative institutions he helped strengthen. His work contributed to the smooth integration of Hyderabad-Karnataka into modern India, the promotion of equitable governance, and the development of parliamentary traditions. Today, he is remembered as a symbol of integrity, commitment, and principled leadership.

Anecdotes and Public Perception

Colleagues described him as humble, approachable, and deeply committed to public welfare. During parliamentary debates, he was known to speak with clarity, referencing legal frameworks and historical precedents. In his constituency, people often recounted his personal visits to villages, listening to grievances, and ensuring that government schemes reached the marginalized.

ಜೀವನ ಚರಿತ್ರೆ (ಕನ್ನಡ)

ಪರಿಚಯ

ಜಗನ್ನಾಥ ರಾವ್ ವೆಂಕಟ ರಾವ್ ಚಂದ್ರಿಕಿ (16 ಆಗಸ್ಟ್ 1916 – 20ನೇ ಶತಮಾನ) ಭಾರತೀಯ ಸ್ವಾತಂತ್ರ್ಯ ಹೋರಾಟಗಾರ, ವಕೀಲ, ಸಚಿವ ಮತ್ತು ಸಂಸತ್ ಸದಸ್ಯರಾಗಿದ್ದರು. ಹೈದರಾಬಾದ್ ರಾಜ್ಯ ಮತ್ತು ನಂತರ ಮೈಸೂರು ರಾಜ್ಯ (ಇಂದಿನ ಕರ್ನಾಟಕ) ರಾಜಕೀಯ, ಸಾಮಾಜಿಕ ಮತ್ತು ಕಾನೂನು ಅಭಿವೃದ್ಧಿಯಲ್ಲಿ ಅವರ ಪಾತ್ರ ಬಹುಮುಖ್ಯ.

ಇತಿಹಾಸದ ಹಿನ್ನೆಲೆ

ಬ್ರಿಟಿಷ್ ಕಾಲದಲ್ಲಿ ಮತ್ತು ನಿಜಾಮರ ಆಡಳಿತದ ವೇಳೆ ಹುಟ್ಟಿದ ಅವರು, ಹೈದರಾಬಾದ್ ಪ್ರದೇಶದ ಸಾಮಾಜಿಕ ಅಸಮತೋಲನ ಮತ್ತು ರಾಜಕೀಯ ದಮನವನ್ನು ನೇರವಾಗಿ ಕಂಡರು. 20ನೇ ಶತಮಾನದಲ್ಲಿ ಸಾರ್ವಜನಿಕ ಶಿಕ್ಷಣ ಕಡಿಮೆಯಾಗಿತ್ತು ಮತ್ತು ರಾಜಕೀಯ ಸ್ವಾತಂತ್ರ್ಯ ಕಡಿಮೆಯಾಗಿತ್ತು. ಈ ಪರಿಸರವು ಚಂದ್ರಿಕಿಯವರು ನ್ಯಾಯ ಮತ್ತು ದೇಶಭಕ್ತಿ ಭಾವನೆಗಳನ್ನು ಬೆಳೆಸಲು ಪ್ರೇರೇಪಿಸಿತು.

ಬಾಲ್ಯ ಮತ್ತು ಕುಟುಂಬ

ಅವರು ಗುಲ್ಬರ್ಗಾ ಜಿಲ್ಲೆಯ ಚಂದ್ರಿಕಿ ಗ್ರಾಮದಲ್ಲಿ ಶ್ರೀ ವೆಂಕಟ ರಾವ್ ಚಂದ್ರಿಕಿಯವರ ಪುತ್ರರಾಗಿ ಜನಿಸಿದರು. ತಂದೆ ಮತ್ತು ತಾಯಿ ಅವರಲ್ಲಿ ಶಿಸ್ತಿನ, ನೈತಿಕ ಮೌಲ್ಯಗಳ ಮತ್ತು ಸಾರ್ವಜನಿಕ ಸೇವೆಯ ಮೌಲ್ಯಗಳನ್ನು ಬೆಳೆಸಿದರು. ಕುಟುಂಬವು ತಮ್ಮ ಶಿಕ್ಷಣದ ಹಿನ್ನೆಲೆ ಮತ್ತು ಸಮಾಜ ಸೇವಾ ನಿಷ್ಠೆಗಾಗಿ ಗೌರವ ಪಡೆದಿತ್ತು.

ಶಿಕ್ಷಣ

ಅವರು ಸ್ಥಳೀಯ ಶಾಲೆಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ಶಿಕ್ಷಣ ಪಡೆದ ನಂತರ, ನಾಗ್ಪುರ ವಿಶ್ವವಿದ್ಯಾಲಯದಲ್ಲಿ ಬಿ.ಎ. ಪದವಿ ಪಡೆದರು ಮತ್ತು ಓಸ್ಮಾನಿಯಾ ವಿಶ್ವವಿದ್ಯಾಲಯದಲ್ಲಿ ಎಲ್.ಎಲ್.ಬಿ. ಪದವಿ ಸಂಪೂರ್ಣಗೊಳಿಸಿದರು. ಕಾನೂನು ಅಧ್ಯಯನವು ಅವರ ರಾಜಕೀಯ ಮತ್ತು ಆಡಳಿತ ಜೀವನಕ್ಕೆ ಬಲವಾದ ಅಡಿಪಾಯವಾಯಿತು.

ಸ್ವಾತಂತ್ರ್ಯ ಹೋರಾಟದಲ್ಲಿ ಪಾಲ್ಗೊಳ್ಳುವಿಕೆ

1942ರ ಕ್ವಿಟ್ ಇಂಡಿಯಾ ಚಳವಳಿಯಲ್ಲಿ ಚಂದ್ರಿಕಿ ಸಕ್ರಿಯವಾಗಿ ಪಾಲ್ಗೊಂಡರು. ನಿಜಾಮ್ ಆಡಳಿತದಲ್ಲಿ ರಾಷ್ಟ್ರೀಯ ಚಳವಳಿಗಳು ಅಪಾಯಕಾರಿ ಎಂದು ಪರಿಗಣಿಸಲ್ಪಟ್ಟವು. ಅವರು ಗುಪ್ತ ಸಭೆಗಳನ್ನು ಆಯೋಜಿಸಿ ಯುವಜನರನ್ನು ಪ್ರೇರೇಪಿಸಿದರು ಮತ್ತು ಸ್ವಾತಂತ್ರ್ಯದ ಪರ ಹೋರಾಡಿದರು. ಇದರಿಂದ ಬಂಧನ ಮತ್ತು ಕಾರಾಗೃಹ ಅನುಭವಗಳು ಅವರು ಗೌರವ ಹೊಂದಲು ಕಾರಣವಾಯಿತು.

ವಕೀಲ ವೃತ್ತಿ

ಅವರು ವಕೀಲರಾಗಿಯೂ ಸೇವೆ ಸಲ್ಲಿಸಿದರು. ನ್ಯಾಯ, ನೈತಿಕತೆ, ಮತ್ತು ಸಮಾಜದ ಹಿತದ ಪರ ಕಾರ್ಯನಿರ್ವಹಿಸುವುದು ಅವರ ವೈಶಿಷ್ಟ್ಯವಾಗಿತ್ತು. ಅವರು ಸಮಾಜದ ಹಿಂದುಳಿದವರ ಹಕ್ಕುಗಳಿಗಾಗಿ ಹೋರಾಡಿದರು.

ಹೈದರಾಬಾದ್ ರಾಜ್ಯದಲ್ಲಿ ರಾಜಕೀಯ ಜೀವನ

ಸ್ವಾತಂತ್ರ್ಯದ ನಂತರ, ಅವರು ಹೈದರಾಬಾದ್ ರಾಜ್ಯ ವಿಧಾನಸಭೆಗೆ ಆಯ್ಕೆಯಾದರು (1951–1956). ಕಾನೂನು ಮತ್ತು ದತ್ತಿ ಸಚಿವರಾಗಿ, ಅವರು ಧಾರ್ಮಿಕ ದತ್ತಿಗಳನ್ನು ಸುಧಾರಿಸಿದರು ಮತ್ತು ಆಡಳಿತ ವ್ಯವಸ್ಥೆಯನ್ನು ಪಾರದರ್ಶಕತೆಗೊಳ್ಳಲು ಸಹಾಯ ಮಾಡಿದರು.

ರಾಜ್ಯ ಪುನರ್ ಸಂಘಟನೆಯ ನಂತರ

1956ರಲ್ಲಿ ರಾಜ್ಯ ಪುನರ್ ಸಂಘಟನೆಯ ನಂತರ, ಮೈಸೂರು ರಾಜ್ಯದಲ್ಲಿ ಎಕ್ಸೈಸು, ಕಾರ್ಮಿಕ ಮತ್ತು ಸಣ್ಣ ಕೈಗಾರಿಕೆಗಳ ಸಚಿವರಾಗಿದ್ದರು. ಅವರು ಹಿಂದುಳಿದ ಪ್ರದೇಶಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ಉದ್ಯಮ ಅಭಿವೃದ್ಧಿ, ಕಾರ್ಮಿಕ ಕ್ಷೇಮ, ಮತ್ತು ಉದ್ಯೋಗ ಸೃಷ್ಟಿಯನ್ನು ಪ್ರೋತ್ಸಾಹಿಸಿದರು.

ಲೋಕಸಭಾ ಸದಸ್ಯ

1962ರಲ್ಲಿ, ಅವರು ರಾಯಚೂರು ಕ್ಷೇತ್ರದಿಂದ 3ನೇ ಲೋಕಸಭೆಗೆ ಆಯ್ಕೆಯಾದರು. ಸಂಸತ್ತಿನಲ್ಲಿ ಕಾನೂನು, ಕಾರ್ಮಿಕ, ಮತ್ತು ಆರ್ಥಿಕ ಅಭಿವೃದ್ಧಿ ವಿಷಯಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ಚರ್ಚೆಗಳಲ್ಲಿ ಭಾಗವಹಿಸಿದರು. ಅವರ ಉದ್ದೇಶ ಸಾಮಾನ್ಯ ಜನರ ಹಿತಚಿಂತನೆ ಮತ್ತು ಸಮಾನತೆಗಾಗಿ ಕೆಲಸ ಮಾಡುವುದು.

ವೈಯಕ್ತಿಕ ಜೀವನ

1938ರಲ್ಲಿ ಅವರು ವಿಮಲಾಬಾಯಿ ಅವರನ್ನು ವಿವಾಹವಾದರು. ಒಬ್ಬ ಮಗ ಮತ್ತು ಮೂರು ಪುತ್ರಿಯರು ಇದ್ದರು. ಸರಳ ಜೀವನ ಮತ್ತು ಕುಟುಂಬಕ್ಕೆ ಸಮರ್ಪಿತ ಜೀವನವು ಅವರ ವೈಶಿಷ್ಟ್ಯವಾಗಿತ್ತು. ಇತಿಹಾಸ, ಕಾನೂನು, ಮತ್ತು ರಾಜಕೀಯ ಚಿಂತನೆಗಳ ಓದುವಿಕೆ ಅವರ ಹವ್ಯಾಸವಾಗಿತ್ತು.

ನಾಯಕತ್ವ ತತ್ತ್ವ ಮತ್ತು ಧಾರ್ಮಿಕ ಚಿಂತನೆ

ಅವರ ತತ್ತ್ವ ಶಿಸ್ತಿನ ಆಡಳಿತ, ಸಾರ್ವಜನಿಕ ಸೇವೆ, ಮತ್ತು ಸಂವಿಧಾನಾತ್ಮಕ ತತ್ತ್ವಗಳ ಮೇಲೆ ಆಧಾರಿತವಾಗಿತ್ತು. ಶಕ್ತಿ ಸಮಾಜದ ಸೇವೆಗೆ ಸಾಧನವಾಗಿದೆ ಎಂಬುದು ಅವರ ನಂಬಿಕೆ.

ಪಾರಂಪರ್ಯ

ಚಂದ್ರಿಕಿಯವರ ಕಾರ್ಯಗಳು ಹೈದರಾಬಾದ್-ಕರ್ನಾಟಕದ ಏಕತೆಯನ್ನು ಬಲಪಡಿಸಿತು, ನ್ಯಾಯಪಾಲನೆ ಮತ್ತು ಆಡಳಿತ ವ್ಯವಸ್ಥೆಯ ಸುಧಾರಣೆಗೆ ಕಾರಣವಾಯಿತು. ಅವರು ಸದಾ ನೈತಿಕತೆ, ಸಮಾಜ ಸೇವೆ, ಮತ್ತು ನ್ಯಾಯತೀತ ನಾಯಕತ್ವದ ಸಂಕೇತವಾಗಿದ್ದಾರೆ.
